# 🎓 NextStep - Final Project (CADT GEN11 Y2T3 SEG3)

## 📌 Project Description

**NextStep** is a web-based educational discovery and guidance platform designed to help Cambodian high school students easily explore universities, academic majors, and scholarship opportunities in one centralized system.

In Cambodia, many students often struggle to find clear and reliable information about higher education options. Important details such as admission requirements, tuition fees, available programs, campus locations, and scholarship opportunities are usually scattered across different university websites, social media pages, and informal sources. This makes it time-consuming and difficult for students to compare options and make well-informed decisions about their academic future.

**NextStep** aims to solve this problem by providing a single, organized, and user-friendly platform where students can:

  - Discover universities and institutions in Cambodia and beyond
  - Browse different academic majors and programs
  - Explore available scholarships and eligibility requirements
  - View key details such as tuition fees, admission criteria, and deadlines
  - Make more informed decisions about their future studies

## 🚀 Key Features

- 🔐 User Registration and Login (Student & Admin)
- 🔎 University Search and Filtering
- 🎓 Scholarship Search and Filtering
- 📄 University and Scholarship Detail Pages
- ⭐ Personalized Recommendations (Universities & Scholarships)
- 📊 Popular Major Insights
- ❤️ Favorites and Bookmark System
- 🗣️ Peer Rating and Review System
- 🛠️ Administrator Dashboard
- 📡 Core API with Search & Recommendation Algorithms
- 🗄️ Data Management (Universities, Programs, Scholarships, Users)
- 📱 Fully Responsive User Interface

---

## 🧠 Technologies Used

- Frontend: ReactJS
- Backend: NodeJS + ExpressJS
- Database: MySQL

---

## 🎯 Project Goal

NextStep focuses on reducing information gaps and helping students plan their educational pathways more effectively. By centralizing fragmented data into one system, NextStep helps students save time, reduce confusion, and gain confidence in choosing the right academic direction. Ultimately, NextStep aims to empower Cambodian students with better access to education information and support them in taking the next step toward their academic and career goals.
